Tired of Sweepstake Spreadsheets?

Introducing "GoalFlow" the football sweepstakes system. If you have ever managed a sweepstake in the past manually you know it can sometimes be a long and difficult job but it doesn't have to be.

Our Features

561 LEAGUES AND CUPS

Pick teams from over 561 real leagues and cups to customise the sweepstake to your hearts content.

WEEKLY RESULTS EMAILS

Emails are sent out to the group with the results of each week.

CARD PAYMENTS ACCEPTED

Takes players entry fees by card in order to make payments securely, faster and easier.

PAYMENT TRACKING DASHBOARD

Track player payments to stay organised and ensure the prize pool contains the amount it should.

GLOBAL SCOREBOARD

Global scoreboard to show the top players in the world!

GROUP SCOREBOARD

Scoreboard to track who is doing the best within your group.

PICK REMINDER EMAILS

Weekly reminder emails to pick the teams for this week.

NO REGISTRATION FOR PLAYERS

Just sign someone up via email to be part of your sweepstake with no need for players creating accounts.

The Rules

Add up to 4 leagues at a time and start selecting teams.

  • PICK ONE TEAM EACH WEEK - Each week a player picks a single team from the current fixtures which they think will win their game.
  • CAN'T PICK AN OPPOSING TEAM OF ANOTHER PLAYER - It's first come first serve and you can't pick an opposing team to anyone who has picked before in your group. This means you can't directly bet against another player.
  • SAME TEAM CAN'T BE PICKED - The same team can't be picked twice in any single season, to allow for more interesting and unpredictable results.
  • SCORE POINTS EACH WEEK - Points each week are calculated with 3 points for a win, 1 for a draw and 0 for a loss.
